Most stacked-video pain starts before editing. TakeStack keeps the recording instructions simple so solo creators, classes, choirs, and teams can capture phone videos that are easier to sync.

01

Use one guide

Play the same guide track in headphones so each overdub follows the same timing instead of drifting against a different reference.

02

Give every take a cue

03

Keep it vertical and local
